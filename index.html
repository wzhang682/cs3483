Welcome to the Interactive Image Viewer

Follow the instructions below to navigate and interact with the image.

Viewing the image: Press 'v' to enter view image mode. A blurred version of the image will be displayed. Move your index finger in the camera view to see a clear area around the position indicator.

Performing zoom-in: Press 'z' to enter zoom-in mode. The zoom level will be proportional to the distance between your thumb and index finger tips.

Finding persons: Press 'p' to enter find persons mode. The person pointed at by your index finger will be displayed at a larger size.

Finding persons: Press 'f' to enter find faces mode. The person' face pointed at by your index finger will be displayed at a larger size.

Exiting modes: Press 'e' to exit from any special mode and return to the original image display.
